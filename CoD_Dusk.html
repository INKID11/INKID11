In City Of Demons : Dusk, you will be adventuring as a well-known Demon Master, as you have defeated the horrible Horrific Assosciation. However, your troubles are not over yet. You soon discover an abandoned castle which is releasing legions of Horror Demons. You and your new team of Demon Masters are recruited yet again to adventure through the dungeons of the castle and destroy the source. However, these new Horror Demons are stronger then before. Will you make it...
