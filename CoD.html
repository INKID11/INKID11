In City Of Demons, you will be adventuring as an upcoming Demon Master, an extremely prestigious role. Your goal is to capture Saviour Demons to destroy Horror Demons, in order to stop the Horrific Association, whose goal is to destroy the world. You have been recruited by the Saviours Association as a Demon Master for your incredible skill in cathcing, training and using Saviour Demons. You will encounter many friends and enemies, and ultimately attempt to defeat the Horrific Association.
